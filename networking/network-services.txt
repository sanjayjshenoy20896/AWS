AWS PrivateLink:
is a highly available, scalable technology that you can use to privately connect your VPC to services and resources as if they were in your VPC.
It allow communication with AWS services or resources from your private subnets.

Benefits: AWS PrivateLink helps you secure your traffic and connect with simplified management rules.

Use case: It is used for connecting your clients in your VPC to resources, other VPCs, and endpoints.
