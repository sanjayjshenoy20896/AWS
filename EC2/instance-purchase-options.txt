EC2 Instances Purchasing Options
1.) On Demand instances -> Short workloads, predictable pricing ,pay by second
2.) Reserved(1 & 3 yrs) -> Long workloads,convertible reserved intrsnaces
3.) Saving (1 & 3 yrs) -> commitment to amount of usage, long workloads
4.) Spot insances -> Short workloads,cheap, can loose instance with short time
5.) Dedicates hosts -> book an entire physical server,control instance placement
6.) Dediciated instances -> no other customers will share hardware
7.) Capacity Reservations -> Reserve capacity in a specific AZ for any duration
