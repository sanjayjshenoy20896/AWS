Amazon S3 - Static Website hosting
They can host websites and have them accessible on the internet
They webiste url will be (Depending on the region)
Website url will be as follows
http://<bucket-name>.s3-website-<AWS Region>.amazonaws.com
(or)
http://<bucket-name>.s3-website.<AWS Region>.amazonaws.com

if public reads are not enabled, we would get 403 Forbidden error
