Cloud computing
Gives you access to servers,storage,databases and broad set of applications required for the internet

Amazon Web servers
Offers reliable, scalable and inexpensive cloud services and gives you option as free to join as well as pay as you use. This is beneficial

Why AWS?
Locations -> They are spread over multiple regions across the world
Automated backups -> They provide screenshots based backups in automated fashion
Disaster recovery -> They provide service layer aggrements during Disaster recovery
Flexibility and scalability -> scalability of servers and databases.
Pay-as-you-go Pricing model -> Pay for the services as you use them
Security -> They have various encryption and decryption strategy


Benefits
1.) Agility -> Allows us to innovate faster and focus on our valuable IT resources.
2.) Elasticity -> We can provision the amount of services we actually need and we can instantly scale up and down based on need of the business. This prevents over-provisioning of resources.
3.) Deploy global in minutes -> Easy deployment of resources in multiple physical Locations around the world. This helps in maintaining lower latency and less cost.
4.) Pay as you go -> pay only for what you consume.



AWS Free Tier:
it allows people to create free tier account, where there are limits to use a service within the limits to have free account.
